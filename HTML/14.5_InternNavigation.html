Domestic Cats

Domestic cats (Felis catus) have lived alongside humans for thousands of years. They are known for their companionship, agility, and curiosity.

Big Cats

Big cats, including lions, tigers, and leopards, are majestic wild animals. They belong to the genus *Panthera* and are known for their strength and hunting prowess.

Cat Behavior

Cats communicate through body language and vocalizations. Their behaviors, such as purring, kneading, and hunting, are key to understanding their needs and emotions.

Fun Facts

Did you know that cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping? They also have a unique grooming habit that keeps them clean and helps them relax.
