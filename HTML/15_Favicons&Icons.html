All About Cats

Introduction

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their independence, playful behavior, and ability to hunt vermin.

Types of Cats

There are many different breeds of cats, each with its own unique characteristics. Some popular breeds include the Persian, Siamese, and Maine Coon.

Caring for Cats

Caring for a cat involves providing it with food, water, shelter, and regular veterinary care. Cats also need mental and physical stimulation to stay healthy and happy.
